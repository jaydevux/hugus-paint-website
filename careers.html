Careers

Accepting applications for future positions. Please send you curriculum to assistant@huguspaint.com

Painting Foreman

Our ideal candidate must have a minimum of 8 years painting experience. Good demonstrated leadership and strong communication skills is a must. Product knowledge in all aspects of our trade such as: Spraying, Rolling, Read blueprints and ability to plan daily operations. The individual should be conscientious and personable. Reliable transportation is required. Bilingual is most definitely a plus

Drywall

Must have a minimum of five years in drywall. Fits and fastens wallboard or sheetrock into specified position, using hand tools, portable power tools, or adhesive. Cuts metal or wood framing, angle and channel iron, and trim to size, using cutting tools. This individual must have good communication skills and good work ethic. Individual must have reliable transportation. Bilingual is most definitely a plus.

Painter

Our ideal candidate must have a minimum of 5 years painting experience with basic knowledge of a brush and a roll. The individual should be conscientious and personable. Reliable transportation is required. Bilingual is most definitely a plus.

Hangers

Must have a minimum of five years in drywall hanging. Apply cement to backs of tiles and press tiles into place, aligning them with layout marks or joints of previously laid tile. Apply or mount sheetrock to ceilings and walls of buildings to reduce reflection of sound or to decorate room. Measure and cut openings in panels or tiles for electrical outlets, windows, vents using keyhole saws or other cutting tools. Individual must have reliable transportation. Bilingual is most definitely a plus.

Finishers

The primary purpose of a Finisher is that they seals joints between plasterboard or other wallboards to prepare wall surface for painting or papering. Mixes sealing compound by hand or with portable using trowel, broad knife, or spatula. Presses paper tape over joint to embed tape into compound and seal joint or tapes joint, using mechanical applicator that spreads compound and embeds tape in one operation. Workers should be able to: Direct or plan an activity as well as lift heavy materials. Individuals must have reliable transportation. Bilingual is most definitely a plus.
